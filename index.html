Founder & ceo

DiGiTel Spectrum is a spectrum of Customer Engagement Solutions. Our solutions are designed to support the enterprises throughout the complete life-cycle of their products starting from product launch to brand building to marketing for the maturity stage.

We believe that marketing and customer engagement should be based on the product and target audience. Hence we do not endorse ‘one size fits all’ type of solutions. We are continually building new innovative solutions which are effective, custom-build and can support early stage start-ups to large corporates.

Our team has over 45 years of combined experience is sales and marketing in both B2C and B2B for various products and services. Our own experience helps us to analyse customer requirements better and thereby custom build an effective solution.

Our Team

Our core team comprises of expert sales & marketing professionals who have worked with leading companies like Tata Group, Dell India, Reliance Group, AGC Networks, Rashi Peripherals etc. With deep understanding of both consumer and enterprise business, we are able to design custom-made solution which are effective, measurable and suiting every budget.
