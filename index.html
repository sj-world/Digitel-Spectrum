Case Studies

How Geo-Analytical Messaging can help organizations in Brand Promotion

A retail outlet dealing with some aspirational brand of Wrist Watches wanted to promote their newly opened showroom in a prime commercial location of Bangalore. They have also set-up a multi-brand service center for imported watches. The company wants to target the high net-worth individuals (HNIs) living around the showroom or visiting nearby commercial places.

While the traditional marketing campaigns like Radio, Newspaper Ad, flyers etc. are expensive, they are mass advertising media and not specific to the target segment of Company. After evaluating several options, the Company subscribed to our Location Based Marketing Solution: Geo-Analytical Messaging.

We designed a solution for the company to target the specific customer audience based on

•     Geography (within 10 KM radius of the store).
•     Age.
•     Post-paid subscriber with high ARPU.
•     Type of handset used.
•     Roaming activated (Domestic / International).

There are 39 such filters which can be used in any combination to filter the desired target audience for sending SMS, Voice Calls or In-app Banner Ad. Compared to the traditional marketing campaigns which target a universal set of audience, our analytical messaging targets “just the right audience” hence enhancing the RoI of your marketing campaigns many folds. Not only that you can chose your campaign based on your budget and be sure of getting the results.

How Virtual Attendant can help generate revenue

A Private University was unable to convert all enquiries into a meaningful prospect. Although it spent generously on Marketing and Brand Building, it was not able to track the actual enquiries generated through its Ad-campaigns and the management was not sure weather to continue with the campaigns unless it was introduced to the Cloud based Virtual Attendant System.

No amount of Marketing spend can generate business unless it is backed by a reliable infrastructure to handle the interest generated. Some of the major reasons for organizations losing customers are:

•     Unreliable telephone lines.
•     No tracking of missed calls – every missed call is a missed opportunity.
•     Multiple numbers to be published.
•     No tracking of calls during holidays or after office-hours.
•     No monitoring of quality of conversations.
•     No process to monitor the lead generated through Ad-campaigns.

A cloud based Virtual Attendant System was able to address all of the above and much more :

•     Cloud based solution with zero capex.
•     Customized call routing based on geography, language, time of the day, day of the week etc.
•     Auto attendant and Interactive Voice Response.
•     Professional response to your customers.
