Our Solutions: ( Virtual Attendant )

Our cloud based ‘Virtual Attendant” can receive your incoming calls professionally. A “missed call” can be a “missed opportunity”. With a conventional telephony, it is hard to track the calls – answered or missed. An on premise solution requires a high capex, and a basic technical knowhow to manage it. There is still a risk of the communication getting out of order due to several factors like snapped telephone lines, system errors, delayed support etc. A cloud based professional ‘Virtual Attendant” can solve these issues and offer a professional communication medium which can be tracked, monitored and managed well.

Virtual Call Center Solution

With our on-cloud “Virtual Call Center Solution” you are ready to go live immediately without investing on the costly Infrastructure or Maintenance. Our comprehensive Call Center solution offers world-class features of a conventional on-premise solution, in just a fraction of it’s cost.

The solution being on the cloud, you can start whenever you want, expand at will or even drop the services without having to think about your investment. It provides superb flexibility with extreme simplicity in terms of implementation of policies and monitoring the performance of your agents. The agents can be distributed over a geography or they may even work from home. With CRM integration, Open APIs, IVR, Soft Dialler, Burge-in, Recording, Skill based routing, Geography based routing, Time based routing, Holiday messages and many more such features, our Virtual Call Center Solutions can offer you a comprehensive yet a cost effective and flexible solution for call management.

Analytical Messaging

Most conventional marketing tools are either expensive or ineffective. Most of the mailers you send would go to the SPAM mail box. Flyers have limited reach due to difficulty in distribution, whereas newspaper ads are expensive just like TV or Radio advertisements.

Bulk messages reaches everyone but effectiveness is really low because the receiver is either not your target customer or it is simply not read. However, you would still be paying for all these efforts. A smarter way of messaging is “Analytical Messaging” where you can filter your target audience by number of filters like Geography (Pin Codes), Age, Gender, Device type, Average spend on communication and many more. With a combination of filters, you can deliver your message to the right audience, thereby increasing the chances of catching attention of the reader. Your overall spend comes down as you target only your prospective customers and at the same time, the effectiveness of your campaign increases.
