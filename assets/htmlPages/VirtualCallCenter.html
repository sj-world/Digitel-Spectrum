Virtual Call Center Solution

With our on-cloud “Virtual Call Center Solution” you are ready to go live immediately without investing on the costly Infrastructure or Maintenance. Our comprehensive Call Center solution offers world-class features of a conventional on-premise solution, in just a fraction of it’s cost. The solution being on the cloud, you can start whenever you want, expand at will or even drop the services without having to think about your investment.

It provides superb flexibility with extreme simplicity in terms of implementation of policies and monitoring the performance of your agents. The agents can be distributed over a geography or they may even work from home.

With CRM integration, Open APIs, IVR, Soft Dialler, Burge-in, Recording, Skill based routing, Geography based routing, Time based routing, Holiday messages and many more such features, our Virtual Call Center Solutions can offer you a comprehensive yet a cost effective and flexible solution for call management.
